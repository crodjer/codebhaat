Our motto ...

Code#Bhaat is a concept concieved by a few n00bs of Kgp about 3 years back. It has been in and out of action, until now. We have always tried to promote Algorithm Intensive coding in our college and build a guild around it. Ofcourse it has not been a very successful attempt, Code#Bhaat finally presents a platform where KGPians can not only SOLVE questions but MAKE their own statements! Give and take is the essence of any sustainable society.

What's in there for you ?

With Code#Bhaat you get to participate and compete with your peers. It's a tough world outside and all we do is to make your ready for the big fight :). The incentive are prizes meant only for KGPians. Infact there'll be separate prizes for various years!

Does it improve your profile ?

What Code#Bhaat provides is not only to solve questions, but to create them. Making new questions has a very different thrill, you generally need to be careful of boundary cases. Boasitng about it in your CV does create a strong impact on the reader.
